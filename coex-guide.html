The Conference @ COEX

Getting to COEX and eating around the venue.

Getting Here

  • Line 2 — Samseong Station, Exit 5 or 6 (directly connected underground)
  • Line 9 — Bongeunsa Station, Exit 7
  • Line 7 — Cheongdam Station, Exit 2 (~10 min walk)

Useful links: COEX official site · Open in Google Maps · Floor directory

Eating Near the Venue

COEX Mall (Starfield) has a large food court in B1 and numerous restaurants on multiple floors. For a broader selection, the streets around Samseong and Bongeunsa stations have many options.

COEX Food Court — B1
Inside Starfield COEX Mall, basement level
Large Korean food court with bibimbap, kimbap, noodles, and more. Kiosk ordering. Fast, affordable (8,000–12,000 KRW). Busy at lunch during conference days — go before 12 or after 1:30.
Fast Affordable In-venue
Bongeunsa-ro Restaurant Strip
5 min walk from Bongeunsa Station Exit 7
A street with many Korean restaurants ranging from BBQ to soup to noodles. Quieter than the mall and more local. Walk and pick what looks busy — that's usually the sign of a good place.
Local Variety
GS25 / CU / 7-Eleven (in-building)
Multiple locations inside COEX
Korean convenience store food is genuinely good — triangle kimbap, onigiri, instant ramen, sandwiches, hot food. Fast and cheap (2,000–5,000 KRW). Good option between sessions.
Budget 24/7

In the COEX Mall

  • Starfield Library — Iconic free library inside the mall. Good spot to sit and read between sessions.
  • Aquarium — COEX Aquarium on B1 if you need a break from research.
  • CGV Cinema — For evening downtime. Many screenings have English subtitles.
  • Olive Young — Inside the mall. Pick up skincare, OTC meds, or snacks.
